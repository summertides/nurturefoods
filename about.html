ABOUT NURTURE

Nurture is a non-profit organization founded in 1925 by Williams James G. Partfield III. Partfield was a sweet old man that believed in equality for all and supplied food to many many people. Partfield and his wife founded Nurture as an easier way to connect with the citizens and give them power through food.
